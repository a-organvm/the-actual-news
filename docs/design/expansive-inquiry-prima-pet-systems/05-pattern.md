## Topological Reading

The design topology is a three-layer field:

- Surface: a lean phasing body with visible inner voids and programmable particle texture.
- Behavior: ambient expression of task and system state through tempo, density, posture, and light.
- Governance: strict boundaries around what the pet observes, implies, packages, and exports.

Prima is compelling because those layers contradict each other cleanly. The surface wants indeterminacy. Behavior needs readability. Governance requires precision. The design succeeds if all three remain visible at once.
