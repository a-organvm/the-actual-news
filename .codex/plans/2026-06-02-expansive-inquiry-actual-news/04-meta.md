## System Diagnosis

Scope narrowed the question from "news platform" to "public-record substrate with a news interface." Logic exposed the decisive implementation gap: the repo has a protocol and Phase 0 substrate but lacks the complete civic reporting loop. Mythos identified the emotional/institutional archetype: not heroic disclosure, but durable record-keeping. Bridge translated the idea into operational domains where trust is produced by logs, gates, audits, and interconnection standards.

The lenses disagree productively. Logic wants a runnable end-to-end system. Mythos wants a civic institution. Bridge wants auditability standards. The synthesis should not choose one; it should define the next milestone as a runnable civic record artifact that proves the institution and the standard at once.

## Feedback Loop

1. Regenerate Scope after a concrete target story is chosen, ideally a local civic beat story with primary sources.
2. Re-run Logic against the chosen story to derive the minimal end-to-end implementation path.
3. Re-run Bridge only after implementation, comparing the artifact to aviation/bookkeeping/clinical registry controls.
4. Re-run Pattern after a working story bundle exists, because the current pattern is still mostly architectural.

Prune:

- Generic "AI fact checking" framing.
- Any feature that does not improve story bundle integrity, conformance, or civic workflow.
- Broad national/international news ambitions before a local civic loop works.

Regenerate:

- Evidence service around real content hashing and persistence.
- Claim extraction around reviewable proposals, not authoritative model output.
- Public-web around inspectable story bundles, not a generic feed.

## Three Adaptations

1. Treat the next milestone as an artifact, not a feature: one civic story bundle that can be independently replayed from story version, claims, evidence objects, edges, reviews, corrections, and events.
2. Add a "trust substrate matrix" to the roadmap: transparency standard, credibility rating, claim verification, media provenance, correction ledger, economics, and federation. Mark what the repo implements versus delegates.
3. Convert "news as public service" into conformance language: a node earns the claim only when it can pass publish-gate tests, export bundles, preserve corrections, and disclose governance/funding.
